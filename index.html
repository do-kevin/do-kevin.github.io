About Me

I am an UC Davis English graduate training to be a full stack developer.

There are occasions when I am asked what would be my favorite this and that, and my answer will probably be "I don't know." I'm interested in a variety of things, especially when it comes to talking about the the aspect of storytelling in different mediums. You will find that I am enthusistic about videogames and films. I tend to lean towards the sci-fi and fantasy genres, especially Star Wars and Mistborn series. Cyberpunk would be my most favorite subgenre due to its design aesthetic and themes of high tech, low life. I love the designs that go into interpreting the genre from concept art and 3D-models of IPs such as Blade Runner 2049, Deus Ex series, Cyberpunk 2077, Dredd, and System Shock 2.

I am magnetized over stories that have mature aspects and themes, especially when it comes to characters who make decisions that fluctuate between the spectrums of morality and immorality, apathy and empathy, and compromises. I like reading into the backstories of these characters or figuring out their behavioral cues and personality. These would include dark fantasy RPG games such as Fallout: New Vegas or the Witcher game series. I am also keen on how dialogue and staging are shown whether it's the Witcher and Game of Thrones books, HBO's Deadwood TV show, or Quentin Tarotino films.
